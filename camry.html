The Camry, My First Love

2003 Toyota Camry XLE with a 2.4L four cylinder engine

The complete list of modifications contain: Custom Spyder Halo headlights, LED fog, taillight, and interior light conversion, RGB underglow, Pioneer stereo and amplifier, Polk Audio 10 inch subwoofer, and 35% front, 20% rear window tint.
